COI Link Map

Conflict of Interest (COI) is mapped here with every direct link it leads to from the ORIA flowchart. The visualization and table below show the exact COI pathway and its outbound destinations.
  • Blue node = COI main page.
  • Bold nodes = primary COI actions.
  • Red nodes = external links that leave the COI page (including help ticket and email button).
  • Yellow nodes = RAMP training category destinations.

COI Breakdown

  • COI main page is https://coi.gmu.edu/, and it is the hub for disclosure, training, guidance, and FAQ.
  • Submit Your Disclosure or Request Approval in RAMP is the embedded disclosure path. It is a SAML login into RAMP where disclosure forms are submitted and approvals are requested. This is the primary disclosure entrypoint.
  • RAMP COI Training is the training hub at https://ramp.gmu.edu/trainingcoi/. It includes:
    • Submit a Help Ticket
    • Find RAMP Training
    • Contact Us Email Button
  • Training – COI/COC → Find RAMP Training takes you to the general RAMP training listing page at https://ramp.gmu.edu/training/. This page includes the following related training portals:
    • Grants and Agreements Training — https://ramp.gmu.edu/trainingga/
    • Payroll Certification (PayCert) Training — https://ramp.gmu.edu/training-paycert/
    • Conflict of Interest/Conflict of Commitment (COI/COC) Training — https://ramp.gmu.edu/trainingcoi/
    • Safety Training — https://ramp.gmu.edu/training-safety/
    • Institutional Review Board (IRB) Training — https://ramp.gmu.edu/training-irb/
    • Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC) Training — https://ramp.gmu.edu/training-iacuc/
    • MasonLEAPS — https://hr.gmu.edu/masonleaps/
  • Annual Disclosure Guidance is a guidance page at https://coi.gmu.edu/annual-disclosure-guidance/. It points back to the RAMP COI training flow, showing the relationship between disclosure rules and training requirements.
  • Frequently Asked Questions is the FAQ page at https://coi.gmu.edu/frequently-asked-questions/, summarizing common COI procedures.
  • Repetition / repeated destination: the RAMP training destination appears multiple times as both a direct training link and as a referenced target from Annual Disclosure Guidance and COI support paths.
